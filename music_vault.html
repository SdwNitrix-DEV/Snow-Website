🎶 Music (out of order)

Snow auto-melts from empty Voice Channels after 10 minutes to save resources.

-play [song/link] Summon music from YouTube or SoundCloud! By default, I'll check the YouTube drifts, but you can paste a direct URL or force a specific platform search by typing sc: or yt: before your song name.
-search [query] Peek through the frost! I'll show the top 5 matches from YouTube or SoundCloud (use sc: or yt: to choose your path). React with a number button to instantly add that track to the queue!
-np Peeks at the current frost-bitten track! Shows song details, artwork, and who started the party.
-queue Peeks at the next 5 tracks waiting to be un-frozen in your list.
-clearqueue Wipes the waiting list clean like a fresh snowfall, but Snow will remain in VC.
-loop Creates an endless winter cycle. Keeps the current list recycling.
-skip / -stop Cast a 30s vote to bypass the flurry or shut down the music entirely.
-pause / -resume Flash-freeze the music temporarily or let the rhythm flow again.
