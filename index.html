❄️ Snow Bot

➕ Invite Snow to Your Server

A new discord bot for moderation, small activities and music commands, made by a small developing team.

🎶 Music Commands

-play [song name/link] Joins your voice channel and plays high-quality audio from YouTube.
-pause / -resume Pauses the current track or keeps the party going.
-skip Votes to skip the current song to the next one in queue.
-np Shows an embed of the song currently playing.
-stop Clears the queue and makes the bot leave the voice channel.

🛡️ Moderation (Staff Only)

-kick @user [reason] Kicks a member from the server. Requires Kick permissions.
-ban @user [reason] Permanently bans a member. Requires Ban permissions.
-clear [amount] Deletes a specific number of messages (Max 100).
-timeout @user [minutes] Prevents a user from typing or joining VC for a set time.

📊 Activity & Utility

-help Displays the interactive help menu in Discord.
-poll "Question" opt1 opt2 Creates a reaction-based poll for your community.
-quiz "Question" answer opt1 opt2 Starts a mini-game quiz in the channel.
