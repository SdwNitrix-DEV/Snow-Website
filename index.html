⚙️ Utility

Need a hand? Snow is here to show you the way through the frost!

-help Opens Snow's help manual. Use -help [command] for a deeper look into her powers.

🛡️ Moderation (Staff Only)

⚠️ These commands require specific permissions (Kick, Ban, or Manage Messages).

/setlogs [#channel] Sets the destination for Snow's **"Audit Blizzard."** Leave blank to disable logs.
/clear [amount] Sweeps away up to 100 messy messages like a fresh snowstorm.
/kick @user [reason] Escorts a member out into the snow and logs the event to the mod-logs
/ban @user [reason] Puts a member on **"Permanent Ice."** Bans them forever and logs the action.
/timeout @user [mins] [reason] Flash-freezes a user's ability to chat for a set amount of time.
-say [message] Let Snow be your voice! Makes the bot repeat your message.

🎮 Activity

-poll "Question" "Opt1" "Opt2" "Opt3" "Opt4" Start a 2-minute 'Snowy Showdown.' Toss out 2 to 4 options and see which one the server picks!
/quiz [question] [answer] A 60-second **"Blizzard Brain"** trivia race. First one to find the exact answer wins!

👾 Characters

Freeze your favorite characters into Snow's memory and let them speak through the blizzard!

/char_register [name] [file] Invite a new friend into Snow's gallery! Upload an image to save a permanent character to your library.
  • Stored safely in Snow's frozen archives forever.
/cshout [name] [message] 5m CD Cast a "Voice Swap" spell! Speak as your saved character using a temporary webhook.
  • Snow keeps a watchful eye on all shouts for server safety.

🎶 Music

Snow auto-melts from empty Voice Channels after 10 minutes to save resources.

-play [song/link] ummon a track from the blizzard or add it to your frosty queue.
-search [query] Sifts through the snow for 5 YouTube results. Type 1-5 to pick your favorite.
-np Peeks at the current frost-bitten track! Shows song details, artwork, and who started the party.
-queue Peeks at the next 10 tracks waiting to be un-frozen in your list.
-clearqueue Wipes the waiting list clean like a fresh snowfall, but Snow will remain in VC.
-loop Creates an endless winter cycle. Keeps the current list recycling.
-skip / -stop Cast a vote to bypass the current flurry or shut down the music entirely.
-pause / -resume Flash-freeze the music temporarily or let the rhythm flow again.
