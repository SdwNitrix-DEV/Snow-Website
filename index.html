➕ Invite Snow to Your Server

A free discord bot for moderation, small activities and music commands. Snow Bot supports both - and / prefixes for all commands!

On this web you can see the whole list of available commands. Also, you can manually invite the Snow Bot to your server by pressing the button above.

⚙️ Utility

-help Shows the general command list. Use -help [command] for deep details.

🛡️ Moderation (Staff Only)

⚠️ These commands require specific permissions (Kick, Ban, or Manage Messages).

/setlogs [#channel] Sets the destination for moderation logs. Leave blank to disable.
-clear [amount] Deletes up to 100 messages in the current channel.
/kick @user [reason] Removes a member and logs the action to the mod-log channel.
/ban @user [reason] Permanently bans a member and logs the action.
/timeout @user [mins] [reason] Prevents a user from talking for a set time.
-say [message] Makes the bot repeat your text.

🎮 Activity

-poll "Question" "Opt1" "Opt2" ... Starts a 2-minute vote. Max 6 options. (5m Cooldown)
-quiz "Question" "Answer" "Wrong" ... Starts a 60-second trivia game. (10m Cooldown)

🎶 Music

Auto-leaves empty Voice Channels after 10 minutes to save resources.

-play [song/link] Plays music from YouTube or adds it to the queue.
-search [query] Finds 5 YouTube results. Type 1-5 to choose.
-np / -nowplaying Shows a beautiful embed of the song currently playing.
-queue Shows the next 10 songs waiting to be played.
-clearqueue Wipes the waiting list but stays in the VC.
-loop Toggles repeat mode. Keeps the current list recycling.
-skip / -stop Vote to skip the current track or stop the bot entirely.
-pause / -resume Temporarily stop the music or keep the party going.
