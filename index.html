🛡️ Moderation (Staff Only)

⚠️ These commands require specific permissions (Kick, Ban, or Manage Messages).

❄️ Notice: Keep Snow's role above other members in your Role List so she has the "high ground" to moderate!

-setlogs [#channel] Sets the destination for Snow's 'Audit Blizzard'. Leave blank to disable logs.
-clear [amount] Sweeps away up to 100 messy messages like a fresh snowstorm.
-kick @user [reason] Escorts a member out into the snow and logs the event to the mod-logs
-ban @user [reason] Puts a member on 'Permanent Ice'. Bans them forever and logs the action.
-timeout @user [mins] [reason] Flash-freezes a user's ability to chat for a set amount of time.
-untimeout @user [reason] Thaw out a flash-frozen user early! Strict Mod Rule: Only the original moderator who cast the freeze (or a Server Admin) has the power to lift it.
-say [message] Let Snow be your voice! Makes the bot repeat your message.

🎧 Voice Moderation (Staff Only)

Tools for the moderation team to control the howling winds and keep the voice channels peaceful.

-vcmute [@user] [reason] 'Flash-freeze' a user's microphone! The ice is too thick for them to break on their own, ensuring the blizzard stays quiet. All strikes are recorded in the mod logs.
-vcunmute [@user] [reason] Melt the ice from a user's microphone, allowing their voice to carry through the winter breeze once again!
-vcdeafen [@user] [reason] Encase a user's headset in solid ice! They won't be able to hear any of the chatter in the voice channel until a moderator thaws them out.
-vcundeafen [@user] [reason] Shatter the ice around a user's headset, unblocking their ears and bringing them safely back into the conversation.

🎭 Character Vault

Freeze your favorite characters into Snow's memory and let them speak through the blizzard!

-char_register [name] [file] Invite a new friend into my gallery! Your frosty vault can hold a maximum of 4 characters. Registering an existing name will update their picture!
  • Stored safely in Snow's frozen archives forever.
-char_delete [name] Shatter a character's frozen memory. It'll give you a 45-second 'Safety Thaw' to confirm there are no icy accidents!
-force_delete_char [@user] [char_name] Admin Only: Bring down the avalanche! Bypass a user's frozen vault and instantly shatter an inappropriate character. No safety thaws, and the strike is logged in the blizzard to keep the server safe.
-char_list Privately peek into your vault to see a list of all your currently saved personas and check your capacity limit.
-cshout [name] [message] 5m CD Cast a 'Voice Swap' spell! Speak as your saved character using a temporary webhook.
  • Snow keeps a watchful eye on all shouts for server safety.

🎶 Music (out of order)

Snow auto-melts from empty Voice Channels after 10 minutes to save resources.

-play [song/link] Summon music from YouTube or SoundCloud! By default, I'll check the YouTube drifts, but you can paste a direct URL or force a specific platform search by typing sc: or yt: before your song name.
-search [query] Peek through the frost! I'll show the top 5 matches from YouTube or SoundCloud (use sc: or yt: to choose your path). React with a number button to instantly add that track to the queue!
-np Peeks at the current frost-bitten track! Shows song details, artwork, and who started the party.
-queue Peeks at the next 5 tracks waiting to be un-frozen in your list.
-clearqueue Wipes the waiting list clean like a fresh snowfall, but Snow will remain in VC.
-loop Creates an endless winter cycle. Keeps the current list recycling.
-skip / -stop Cast a 30s vote to bypass the flurry or shut down the music entirely.
-pause / -resume Flash-freeze the music temporarily or let the rhythm flow again.
